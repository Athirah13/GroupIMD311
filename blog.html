Ultimate Key to Earn Customer's Trust





Before we introduce our products to customers, we need to establish why we should build customers' trust and loyalty in the first place ? This is our first time in doing business which we think the value of products is the most important for us start the business. So, we focus more on providing the best quality and show to world that this is our strength of business. The truth is, people nowadays do not buy things that have 100 percent high quality and because it was a good product. It may have some high quality factor which contribute to 40% and above only. But, people buy the products because they trust the brand or the seller. That means the customers that have been try the products may repeat their buy again as what we said earlier. That may contain 60% on this factor. We will said that for our products, it is not exactly 100 percent high quality but we will try to attract people by provide them a good product which can make them trusts us and buy it again from us. We are not trying to say that high quality is not important, it is just some technique to make the products become more popular and get high value of the products. As we know, nowadays there are many copied products and it have make some entrepreneur need to compete with each other because of the similarity of the products. So, what we do to make sure the customers buy our products ? "By gaining their trust and loyalty." It is means that when our customers trust our product, they will keep continuing to buy and support our products without compare it with another similar products. This is because the customers already satisfy with the quality and the price of our products that we sells to them. Later, we will know how important our great value of product to them and it will bond our relationship more closer with the customers. That is why we need to keep our customers by giving them great value for us build trust and loyalty with them. So, the first step is : To gain our customers' trust. Because trust will lead our customer to loyalty which means that when they trying our products, they will keep repeating and help to promote this product to their relatives and friends as well. When they become loyalty, customer will not have any doubt anymore about our products and can encourage themselves with their families to buy our products again in the future and their daily lives.
